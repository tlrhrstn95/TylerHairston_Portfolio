About Me

Hi, I'm Tyler Hairston, an experienced Workforce Management Specialist and Payroll Systems Administrator with over five years of expertise in workforce solutions, system optimization, and data analytics.

Throughout my career, I have successfully implemented and managed workforce management platforms such as UKG Pro, Kronos, Workday, and Paycom. My expertise spans payroll processing, scheduling, system configuration, data reporting, and compliance. I specialize in creating custom reports within UKG and leveraging Power BI to develop dynamic visualizations that drive data-driven decision-making.

With a proven ability to lead projects and enhance operational efficiency, I have worked across healthcare, corporate, and public sectors. I am passionate about optimizing business processes, improving workforce analytics, and fostering effective communication within teams.

I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to apply my skills in workforce management and data analytics. If you're interested in working together or learning more, feel free to get in touch.
